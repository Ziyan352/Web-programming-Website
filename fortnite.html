About Fortnite

"Fortnite," developed by Epic Games and released in July 2017, is a wildly popular battle royale game known for its vibrant graphics, unique building mechanics, and dynamic gameplay. In Fortnite, up to 100 players compete to be the last person or team standing on a constantly shrinking map, using an array of weapons and resources to build structures for defense and strategy. Available on multiple platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices, Fortnite's accessibility, frequent updates, and cultural tie-ins have helped it maintain a massive, diverse player base and significant influence in the gaming industry.

Key Features:

  • Unique building mechanics
  • Colorful, cartoonish art style
  • Cross-platform play
  • Regular updates and events
