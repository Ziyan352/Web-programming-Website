Some Of The Best Battle Royale Games

PUBG

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation. In the game, up to one hundred players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to kill others while avoiding getting killed themselves.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a free-to-play battle royale game developed and published by Epic Games. Players airdrop onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. The game features building mechanics that allow players to construct walls, obstacles, and other structures.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. The game is set in the same universe as Titanfall and features unique characters with special abilities, adding a new layer of strategy to the battle royale formula.

Call of Duty: Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. The game supports up to 150 players in a single match and includes various modes such as Battle Royale and Plunder.
