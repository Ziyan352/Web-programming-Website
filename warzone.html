About Call of Duty: Warzone

"Call of Duty: Warzone" is a free-to-play battle royale game released on March 10, 2020, by Infinity Ward and Raven Software, published by Activision. It features large-scale, last-player-standing gameplay in the fictional city of Verdansk, with up to 150 players competing in various modes. Warzone is part of the broader Call of Duty franchise and supports cross-platform play, enabling a diverse and extensive player community. The game is known for its intense combat, strategic gameplay, and continuous updates that keep the experience fresh and engaging.

Key Features:

  • Massive map with up to 150 players
  • Integrated with Call of Duty: Modern Warfare
  • Various game modes including Plunder and Battle Royale
  • Cross-platform play
